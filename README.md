##Problem statement:

-Uncertainty in Climate Predictions: Accurate forecasting is challenging due to multiple dynamic and interdependent factors.

-Frequent Extreme Weather Events: Increased occurrence of floods, droughts, cyclones, and wildfires causing environmental and economic damage.

-Air Quality Deterioration: Growing pollution and greenhouse gas emissions negatively impacting human health and ecosystems.

-Complex Climate Data Handling: Climate data is vast, heterogeneous, and difficult to process for meaningful insights.

-Lack of Real-Time Monitoring & Analysis: Limited systems for real-time tracking and predictive analytics of climate changes.
